Important lore fact: Rudi is actually a real dog in real life. The game version is based on the real Rudi, including the black spot on his side, the goofy energy, and the legendary zoomies.

Story: The history of Rudi Foodi began when Beckham Miller decided to create a silly game featuring his real life dog – Rudi. Initially, it was supposed to be an absolutely simple and basic prototype consisting of a single room, some drifting treats, and a ridiculous low poly dog. However, after the release of the prototype, every subsequent addition of features got increasingly weirder and more amusing. At first came "Zoomies" followed by cloning and power-ups, various size modes, rotating levels, dancing, meteors, and flying debris. It seems that every bug Beckham Miller had found in his updates was so ridiculous that he would not remove them but instead integrate it into the story of Rudi. Currently, there are twenty levels in Rudi Foodi ranging from pirate ships, through the lava islands and neon cities all the way to floating cloud worlds, candy forests, factories, caverns, and dog parks of enormous size. Moreover, every level in Rudi Foodi is accompanied by unique music creating distinctive moods for each world. As for Rudi himself, he has evolved from a pixelated character to a smoother and slightly larger version of the actual dog. Nowadays, Rudi Foodi can be described as an inside joke driven by the power of treats, zoomies, friendship, and madness.
